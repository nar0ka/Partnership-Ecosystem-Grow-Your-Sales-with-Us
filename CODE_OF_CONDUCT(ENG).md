# Community Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing an open, friendly, and safe environment for all participants in our affiliate program.

## Our Principles

* **Respect:** We treat each other with respect. Discrimination and harassment will not be tolerated.
* **Integrity:** Transparency in product promotion is essential. The use of spam or prohibited methods (blackhat) will result in exclusion from the program.
* **Collaboration:** We are always ready to support our affiliates with materials and answer any questions.

## Support

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us via the [Issues](https://github.com/nar0ka/Partnership-Ecosystem-Grow-Your-Sales-with-Us) section or on Telegram.
